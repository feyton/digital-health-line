About Us

Your best partner in healthcare services

Digital Healthline Solutions (DHS) is a social enterprise based in Rwanda which targets members of the community with provision of readily access to services related to surveillance, guidance, and treatment throughout the journey to improve quality of care utilizing digital technology incentives.

Digital Healthline Solutions offers Channel network of Non-Communicable Diseases (NCDs) patients, Champions, patient's advocates, health care professionals, Health Educators, public and private health institutions; with strong dedication to act on NCDs, enhance patient education and compliance to treatment plans.

Our Mission

We strive for Patient Health boosting and a world free of non-communicable diseases.

Our Vission

To offer best digital health services in delivery of high-quality healthcare management solutions for patients and improving the safety of patient care.

Our Values

Our Values related on the better services we provide we do it professional, Empathy and Humility and Trustworthy, We prioritize professional skills, great compassion towards our clients and confidentiality in our field of work.
