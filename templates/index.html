Did You Know ?

Usually, hypertension does not come with immediate symptoms. Symptoms may take a long time before they surface, and you may just feel fine. There is a need to get your blood pressure at a constant pace. Get yourself a regular screening

Young people are at risk too!!

Young people can have high blood pressure, too. Nearly 1 in 4 adults aged 20 to 44 have high blood pressure. This is thought to be caused by increasing rates of obesity, and type 2 diabetes. While these conditions are long term, they are preventable and curable. Avoid risk factors that can lead to High blood pressure!!

Learn more Contact Us
Women are at risks

Women are at a considerable risk for future complications of pregnancy e.g., preeclampsia. Mostly, African, and African American women are one of the ethnic groups with this risk mainly due to obesity.

Learn more Contact Us
Did you know you can reduce chance of having life threatening conditions due to hypertension?

High blood pressure increases the risk for heart disease and stroke. Blood pressure is the pressure which constantly pushes blood through the lumen of your arteries for transportation. If it stays high, it can damage your heart and cause significant health complications.

Medication Surveillance

We offer a comprehensive informed medication tracking, planning and compliance using patient centered care approach which allows a user to interact with his/ her medications panel, adjust the time to take medicines, get reminders and a generalized report for each treatment.

Health Educator

Appointments Management system

NCD Screening

A growing number of people with Non communicable disease are unaware. DHS brings the solution closer to the audience timely for screening and prevention of lifethreatning comorbidities. We shall be working with our mobile medical team to get screening at patient’s place. Get regular updates on important events happening regarding the NCDS Action and Invitations.

Our Customers and beneficiaries

Our customers are Non communicable diseases (NCD) patients with difficulty to comply with medications by automatic reminders, monitoring during dispense of medications plus medical professional advice.
